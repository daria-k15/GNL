Moscow is the capital and largest city of Russia.
The city stands on the Moskva River in Central Russia, with
a population estimated at 12.4 million residents within the city
limits,[12] over 17 million residents in the urban area,[13] and over 20 million residents
in the metropolitan area.[14] The city covers an area of 2,511 square kilometres (970 sq mi),
while the urban area covers 5,891 square kilometres (2,275 sq mi),[13] and the metropolitan area
covers over 26,000 square kilometres (10,000 sq mi).[14] Moscow is among the world's largest cities,
being the largest city entirely in Europe, the largest urban area in Europe,[13] the largest metropolitan
area in Europe,[14] and the largest city by land area on the European continent.[15]
